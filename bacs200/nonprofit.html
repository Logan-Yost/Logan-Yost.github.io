Value Proposition

Value Proposition: Leading the next generation to greater heights

Vision

The University of Northern Colorado aims to provide the best education to scholars looking for a path to their career. UNC looks to give students the opportunity to not only learn in the classroom, but also to gain experience in the workforce to ensure a smooth transition for students as they begin their careers.

Three Strategies

  • Provide students with top tier education
  • Keep students envolved on campus and throughout the community
  • Ensure students with a smooth transition into a career
