Climate Change

Climate Change is a real issue in today's world. Weather it be the alleged global warming caused by humans, or the different weather patterns created by Earth, the climate of this planet is everchanging.The emission of greenhouse gases into the Earth's atmosphere like carbon dioxide are causing Earth's temperatures to rise with a faster pace. Additionally, agriculture and deforestation are known to be helping the cause the issues we have today with climate change.
